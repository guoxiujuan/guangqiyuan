Simple things. Beautiful things. Crazy things. Intuitive things. Things that don't exist yet. Things that make even the most cynical, seen-it-before people say "hey that's pretty cool". We build these things because we love to build. What can we build for you?

Build in Amsterdam was founded by Daan Klaver and Tim Weers, two advertising veterans on a mission to close the gap between design, technology and brilliant ideas. We work in small teams of passionate builders — designers, developers, writers and strategists — to create award-winning work for both local and international brands.

Great experiences occur when relevance, insight, and vision meet. Working side by side with our clients, and based on a deep and thorough understanding of their business and challenges, we help them get perspective, take action and be successful in an ever-changing world.
